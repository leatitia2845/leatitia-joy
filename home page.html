Ritco link people with places


Rwanda Interlink Transport Company (RITCO) is a Public-Private partnership entity that was established to provide sustainable solutions to proliferating public transport problems for the rural, urban population and the region. Established in 2016, Ritco was officially launched on 6th February 2017 with an aim to provide affordable, sufficient and quality intercity connectivity and increase accessibility to developmental activities across the country and region at large.
